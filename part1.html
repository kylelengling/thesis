Step 1: Make your plan!


Being prepared to commute is the best way to start! It's no fun going into it blind and having a miserable time because you were ill prepared or got lost.

So start by checking out the bare essentials and making sure you have everything to get you there and back safely! A quick list would include: a bike (duh), lock, lights, and helmet. Those will get you there and back safely. Everything beyond that just makes the ride that much easier.

So you've got the basic gear gathered. Awesome! Let's take a moment and think about how to commute. I know it's scary and a daunting task, but really it'll all be fine! The first thing you'll want to open your favorite map or mapping website. Google Maps is pretty great for this. Use the map to plan out a route you'll take to your destination. Important factors to consider, traffic, traffic speed, stoplights, bike lanes and other infrastructure. Sometimes the best route isn't the most direct.

Take this time to really consider everything you'll need to do. Does your office have showers you can change in? Are you bringing clothes or leaving a bunch at work? Are you bringing a lunch in your bag? Get used to the idea of carrying most if not all the things you'll need in a backpack or panniers.
